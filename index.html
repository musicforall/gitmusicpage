Mission

music is the people
that make it.

thus far, music narratives have been constrained by linear relations and a fragmented infrastructure defined by protectionism. we believe it is time to think beyond these borders towards a new sense of togetherness and more progressive narratives.

as musicians and music industry insiders, we believe technology achieved a lot for listeners and fans, but not yet enough for the creative side of the value chain. we want to empower the creator and foster collaboration beyond todays limited and linear workflows.

Vision

creativity
of the masses.

music is a collaborative process. one plays the guitar, one brings in the drums and another adds the bass. yet, in the digital age music collaboration is still limited and bound to personal trust and linear workflows. the u protocol, by design, is built to facilitate global collaboration through mechanical trust. by immutably storing ownership and mining standardized metadata in the process of creation, it equips music to travel along new and non-linear routes while at the same time giving creators full control over how their work can be used and commoditized. the protocol provides all necessary data for blockchain enabled royalty distribution and licensing, as well as allowing frictionless integration into the current framework of distribution.
